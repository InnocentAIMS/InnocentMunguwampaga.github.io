Master's Thesis

Dynamics of gradient descent with momentum in linear neural networks
African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS) Senegal
2024-2025

Analyzed convergence and stability using nonlinear evolution equations, highlighting implications for nonlinear AI models. This research contributes to the theoretical understanding of optimization algorithms in deep learning.

Supervisor: Dr. Bruno Sebastian Barton-Singer, University of Crete - FORTH, Greece
